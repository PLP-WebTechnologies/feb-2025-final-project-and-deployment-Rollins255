About Omore Electricals

Learn more about our history, mission, and values

Our Story

Founded by Mr. Phelix Odiwuor (Omore), Omore Electricals began as a small electrical repair shop at Daraja-Mbili Stage in Kisumu. With a passion for quality electrical solutions and excellent customer service, the business quickly grew to become one of the most trusted electrical service providers in the region.

Today, we serve both residential and commercial clients across Kisumu County and neighboring areas, offering a comprehensive range of electrical services and products.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and high-quality electrical solutions while maintaining the highest standards of safety and professionalism.

Our Vision

To be the leading electrical services provider in Western Kenya, recognized for excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Team

Our team consists of certified electricians with years of experience in various aspects of electrical work. We invest in continuous training to ensure our staff stays updated with the latest technologies and safety standards in the electrical industry.
